Venture associate at 886 Studios. I write about technology, culture, and human nature.

I grew up in Southern California, studied business at UC Santa Cruz, and now live in Taipei working as an associate at 886 Studios, alongside the founders of Twitch and Guitar Hero. We’re building an accelerator that backs early stage startups, bringing a slice of Silicon Valley to Asia. Before that, I was a Research Fellow at Contrary Research, where I profiled and wrote about leading startups.

Outside of work, you’ll usually find me watching the Lakers, playing poker, biking around the city, or writing in a cafe.
